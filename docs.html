Documentation

Everything you need to know about using CashBond protocol.

Introduction

CashBond is a decentralized finance (DeFi) protocol built on BNB Smart Chain (BSC) that allows users to earn passive income on their USDT deposits.

  • 1.5% Daily Returns — Earn consistent daily yield on your staked USDT
  • No Admin Keys — Fully immutable with no owner functions
  • Direct Referral Program — Earn up to 15% commission on your direct referrals

How It Works

1

Deposit USDT

Connect your wallet and deposit USDT. 100% of your capital goes to work — zero deposit fees.

2

Earn Rewards

Rewards accrue every second at 1.5% daily (547.5% APR). Claim anytime.

3

Withdraw

Withdraw anytime. No lock period, no daily limits, no withdrawal fees.

Connecting Your Wallet

To use CashBond, you need a Web3 wallet connected to BNB Smart Chain (BSC).

  1. 1 Install MetaMask or any Web3 wallet (Trust Wallet, Rabby, etc.)
  2. 2 Click "Connect Wallet" on the dashboard — the site will auto-switch you to BSC
  3. 3 Make sure you have BNB for gas fees (~$0.10 per transaction) and USDT (BEP-20) to deposit

Making Deposits

Deposit USDT to start earning 1.5% daily yield.

Minimum deposit: $1 USDT

Deposit fee: None — 100% goes to your stake

Approval: First deposit requires a one-time USDT approval (auto-handled)

Optionally, add a referrer address when making your first deposit. Your referrer earns a commission on your ROI rewards — this does not reduce your earnings.

Withdrawing Funds

Withdraw your staked capital at any time — fully liquid, no lock period.

Withdrawal fee: None — 100% returned to you

Lock period: None — withdraw anytime

Daily limit: None — withdraw any amount

When you withdraw, any pending staking rewards are saved and can still be claimed separately.

Claiming Rewards

Click "Claim All Rewards" to collect both your staking ROI and any referral commission earnings in a single transaction.

Staking ROI: Your daily yield from staked USDT

Referral Commission: Earnings from your direct referrals' ROI

How Referrals Work

CashBond has a direct referral program (single level only — NOT multi-level).

How it works: Share your referral link. When someone deposits using your link, you earn a % of their daily ROI rewards.

Min self-stake: You need $50+ staked to earn referral rewards

Active referral: A referral with $50+ staked counts as "active" for tier progression

Single level only: You only earn from your direct referrals, not their referrals

Immutability

The contract is designed to be fully immutable — no one can change how it works after deployment.

  • ✓ Ownership renounced to address(0)
  • ✓ No onlyOwner functions — nothing can be changed
  • ✓ No pause function — cannot be stopped
  • ✓ No admin withdrawals — your funds are safe
  • ✓ No proxy/upgradability — code is final

How Payouts Work

CashBond is a crowd-funded community pool. Payouts come from new deposits entering the protocol. The contract is fully immutable with ownership renounced to address(0).

Important: This is a community-driven protocol. Returns are funded by new deposits into the pool. Only participate with amounts you're comfortable committing.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is CashBond?
A decentralized staking protocol on BNB Smart Chain. Earn 1.5% daily (547.5% APR) on USDT with a direct referral program up to 15%. Fully trustless with no admin keys.
How does the 1.5% daily yield work?
When you deposit USDT, you earn 1.5% of your staked balance every 24 hours. Rewards are calculated per second, so you can claim anytime.
Are there lock periods or withdrawal limits?
No. No lock periods, no withdrawal limits. Deposit and withdraw anytime with no restrictions.
How does the referral program work?
Earn commission on your DIRECT referrals' daily rewards only — single level, not MLM. Rates: Starter 5%, Bronze 8% (3+), Silver 10% (5+), Gold 12% (10+), Diamond 15% (25+ active directs). $1 minimum self-stake required.
Is my money safe?
The contract is immutable — no admin keys, no pause, no upgradability. Ownership renounced to address(0). Deployer private key published. Payouts are funded by new deposits.
How do I claim rewards?
Click "Claim All Rewards" on the dashboard. Claims both staking ROI and referral rewards in a single transaction.
How do I get started?
Connect your Web3 wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet), switch to BNB Smart Chain, have USDT + BNB for gas, then deposit any amount ($1 minimum) to start earning.
What is the minimum deposit?
The minimum deposit is $1 USDT. There is no maximum deposit limit. There are zero deposit fees — 100% of your deposit goes directly to your staked balance.
Can I compound my rewards?
Yes! After claiming your rewards, you can re-deposit them to increase your staked balance and earn a higher daily return. This is a popular strategy for maximizing growth.
Which wallets are supported?
Any Web3 wallet that supports BNB Smart Chain works — including MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Rabby, Coinbase Wallet, and WalletConnect-compatible wallets. The site will prompt you to switch to BSC if needed.
How much BNB do I need for gas?
Transactions on BNB Smart Chain are very cheap — typically around $0.05–$0.15 per transaction. Having $1–$2 of BNB in your wallet is enough for dozens of transactions.
What counts as an "active" referral?
A referral is considered "active" when they have $50 or more staked in the protocol. Only active referrals count toward your commission tier progression.
Do I need to stake to earn referral rewards?
Yes. You need a minimum of $50 staked to be eligible for any referral commission earnings. Below that threshold, referral rewards will not accrue.
Can I change my referrer after depositing?
No. Your referrer is set permanently on your first deposit. It cannot be changed afterward. If you deposit without a referrer, you cannot add one later.
Is this an MLM or pyramid scheme?
No. CashBond uses a single-level direct referral system only. You earn commission from your direct referrals' rewards — you do NOT earn from their referrals. There is no team depth, no levels, and no binary/matrix structure.
What happens if the pool runs low on funds?
This is a community-funded protocol — payouts are sourced from new deposits. If there is insufficient USDT in the pool, claims and withdrawals will revert until the pool is replenished. Only participate with amounts you're comfortable committing.
Can the contract be paused or modified?
No. The contract has no pause function, no admin functions, no proxy/upgradability, and ownership is renounced to address(0). The deployer private key has been publicly shared. The code is final and cannot be changed by anyone.
Are rewards paid in USDT or BNB?
All rewards — both staking ROI and referral commissions — are paid out in USDT (BEP-20) on BNB Smart Chain. You only need BNB for gas fees.
How do referral tiers upgrade?
Your tier upgrades automatically in real-time based on how many active direct referrals you have (each with $50+ staked). There is no manual activation required — the smart contract checks your tier dynamically on every claim.
Where can I verify the smart contract?
The contract is fully verified and open-source on BSCScan. You can read every line of code, check that ownership is renounced, and verify there are no hidden admin functions. Visit the Trust & Security page for the contract address and direct link.
